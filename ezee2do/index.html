Why eZee 2do?

Most to-do apps want you to sign up, subscribe, log in, and learn a workflow. eZee 2do doesn't. You open the app, you type a task, you tap to check it off. That's the whole thing.

Your lists sync privately through your own iCloud account, so what you add on your Mac shows up on your iPhone and iPad before you reach for them. No servers we control, no data we see, no account you have to remember.

✓ Actually simple

Type a task, tap to complete it, swipe to delete. Nothing else to learn.

☁️ Private iCloud sync

Your lists sync between iPhone, iPad, and Mac through your own iCloud. We never see your data.

📱 One purchase, every device

Buy it once. Universal Purchase covers iPhone, iPad, and Mac at no extra cost.

🔔 Live Activities

Watch your list on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island without unlocking your phone.

📋 Multiple lists

Groceries, work, packing — swipe between them on iPhone, pull-down menu on Mac.

🚫 No ads, no tracking

No analytics, no tracking pixels, no third-party SDKs. One price, no surprises.
