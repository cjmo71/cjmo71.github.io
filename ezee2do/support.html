Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use eZee 2do?

No. There is no account to create, no password to remember, and no sign-in screen. Open the app and start typing. If you're signed into iCloud on your Apple devices, your lists will sync between them automatically.

How does sync work?

eZee 2do uses your personal iCloud account to sync. Data is stored in a private iCloud container that only you can access. If iCloud is signed in on both devices, changes made on one device appear on the other within a few seconds.

If sync isn't working, check that iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings → [your name] → iCloud, and that eZee 2do is toggled on in the iCloud app list.

I bought it on Mac. Do I have to buy it again on iPhone?

No. eZee 2do is a Universal Purchase. Buy it once on any platform (iPhone, iPad, or Mac) and it's automatically available on the others at no extra charge, as long as you're signed into the same Apple ID.

To install it on another device: open the App Store on that device, tap your profile icon, and look under Purchased. eZee 2do will be there with a cloud-download button.

Can my family use it too?

Yes. eZee 2do supports Family Sharing, so up to six family members can use the app on their own devices without buying it again.

How do I create a new list?

On iPhone/iPad: tap the list name at the top of the screen to open the lists menu, then tap New List.

On Mac: click the list name in the header, or press ⌘N.

How do I copy a task or an entire list?

On iPhone/iPad: long-press a task to open the copy menu. To copy the whole list, tap the list name to open the menu and choose Copy List.

On Mac: right-click a task for copy options, or highlight part of the task text and right-click to copy just the selection. Use the menu bar icon menu for "Copy Entire List."

What are Live Activities?

On iPhone, you can turn on Live Activities from the menu. Your current list will appear on the Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island (on supported iPhones), so you can see your top tasks without unlocking your phone.

Does eZee 2do work offline?

Yes. The app works fully offline. When you're back online, anything you added or changed syncs automatically.

How do I delete the app's data?

Delete the app to remove local data. To remove cloud copies, sign into iCloud.com, open your Account Settings, find eZee 2do in the Manage app list, and delete its data. Or turn off iCloud for the app in Settings first.

Do you sell my data?

No. We don't collect any data at all, so there's nothing to sell. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

Sync isn't working

Check each of these in order:

  1. Both devices are signed into the same Apple ID.
  2. iCloud Drive is turned on (Settings → [your name] → iCloud → iCloud Drive).
  3. eZee 2do is toggled on in the list of apps using iCloud.
  4. Both devices have working internet.
  5. Try force-quitting the app and reopening it on the device that's out of sync.

The app crashes or won't open

Force-quit the app and reopen it. If that doesn't work, restart the device. If it's still crashing, contact us through the form below with your device model and iOS/macOS version.

I can't dismiss the keyboard on iPhone

Tap the Done button above the keyboard, tap anywhere outside the text field, or switch to a different list. Any of these dismiss the keyboard.

The Mac app disappears when I click outside it

That's expected when you open it from the menu bar icon — it's designed to behave like a popover there. If you want a stable window, launch the app from the Dock, Launchpad, or Spotlight instead. That opens it as a regular window that stays until you close it.
