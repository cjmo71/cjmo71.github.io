Suggest a Feature

Somos InControl · Every idea is read and logged

Somos is built in the open, shaped directly by what its users ask for. There is no formal roadmap — instead, we follow the direction of our users. Your suggestions are important, so please add yours to our list.

Here is what was included from our users so far

  • ✅ Music controls on the Lock Screen — added in build 29.3 · July 16
  • ✅ Single & mismatched surround speakers allowed — added in build 28 · July 12
  • ✅ Stereo-pair balance slider — added in build 26 · July 10
  • ✅ Support for all rooms, not just home theater — added in build 25 · July 8
  • ✅ Surround & subwoofer level control — added early July
  • ✅ Surround on/off toggle — added in build 31 · July 20
  • ◻️ Line-in source selection — first step in build 31 (shows line-in on the right speaker) · selection still in progress
  • ◻️ Find-speaker tone (sound + light flash) — in progress
  • ✅ Create stereo pairs in Somos — added in build 31 · July 20
  • ✅ S1 + S2 split-system support — rebuilt on Sonos household identity · build 32
  • ✅ Faster, more reliable surround & fronts setup — added in build 31 · July 20
  • ✅ Music Playback (Ambient/Full) surround toggle — coming in build 32
  • ✅ Max volume limit per room — coming in build 32
  • ✅ Sliders apply live while you drag (the “volume delay” reports) — coming in build 32

Found a bug instead? Use the support form so it gets triaged as one.

Your message is delivered directly to the developer. No email address is stored on this page. See our Privacy Policy for how we handle contact data.
